Help Center

How do I add a transaction?

Go to Transactions → Add Transaction. Fill in the amount, category, date and description.

How do I set a budget?

Navigate to Budgets, choose a category and set a monthly limit. Progress bars will show your spending.

What are AI Insights?

Our AI (powered by Groq) analyzes your last 30 days of transactions and gives personalized financial advice.

Is my data secure?

Yes! Passwords are hashed, all forms have CSRF protection, and we use SQLAlchemy to prevent SQL injection.
